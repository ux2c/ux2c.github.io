Amber has been a hair stylist for about six years. She has really built a good, loyal client list. Typically woman will go to her because a friend recommended her and usually within a few months they are sending their husband or boyfriend to Amber. She has a couple of clients that she styles the entire family's hair! Amber really likes her job and the people she meets.

During his recent haircut, Bill commented to Amber that he's going to have to find someone in Arizona that will cut his hair like Amber does. He was not real confident he'd be able to do that.

Amber told Bill about Bisicut and asked if he would like her to refer him. Bill said Bisicut sounded too good to be true, but would love a referral and to try it out. Amber said she would send him a referral invite via text messaging as soon as her shift was done.
