Scenario

  • New Client - Specific Stylist

Paul receives an email from his co-worker Curtis with the referral for BISICUT.

He clicks on the link in the email and is brought to a screen that has his name and contact info. on it. Paul verifies the info. is correct. Below that is a place to create a password and enter in his credit card info. Paul does both and is presented with a screen that has a map on it indicating several stylist available within the next hour and his current location.

Since Paul is looking for a specific stylist, so he clicks the "MENU" icon and selects "FIND STYLIST". He types in Nick's full name in the search area and is presented a screen with a brief overview of Nick, including his 4.5 star rating. Paul confirms this is the correct person Curtis recommended to him and decides to book an appointment.

He clicks on "Next Available" and is presented with a nice, clean list of services that Nick offers and the pricing for each service. Paul clicks on "Hair Cut & Beard Trim", then selects the date and time from a calendar and is presented with an overview of his appointment.

Paul verifies the information is correct and clicks "CONFIRM". About a minute later Paul receives a text confirming his appointment with Nick!
