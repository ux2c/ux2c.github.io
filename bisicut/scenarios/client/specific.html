Scenario

  • New Client - Specific Stylist

Paul receives an email from his co-worker Curtis with the referral for Bisicut.

He clicks on the link in the email and is brought to a screen that has his name and contact info. on it. Paul verifies the info. is correct. Below that is a place to create a password and he does.

Paul is then prompted to upload a photo with his hair styled the way he likes it. He does that and then is prompted to input his credit card information. Paul enters in his credit card info. and clicks "SUBMIT".

Paul does and is presented with a "WELCOME SCREEN". There is a button "LOCATE STYLIST", so Paul clicks on it and is presented with a screen that has a map indicating his current location several stylist available within the next hour.

Since Paul is looking for a specific stylist, so he clicks the "MENU" icon and selects "FIND STYLIST". He types in Nick's full name in the search area and is presented a screen with a brief overview of Nick, including his 4.5 star rating. Paul confirms this is the correct person Curtis recommended to him and selects haircut & beard trim from this list of services Nick provides. Paul notices the prices that Nick charges and they seem to be inline with what Paul is used to paying for these services.

He clicks on "NEXT AVAILABLE" and it says Nick's next available appointment is three days from now. That won't work, as Paul will be out of town. Paul then selects an available date and time from a calendar and is presented with an overview of his appointment.

Paul verifies the information is correct and clicks "CONFIRM". About a minute later Paul receives a text confirming his appointment with Nick next week!
